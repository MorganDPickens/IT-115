Welcome to the Characters Page

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is the main character of the movie. He is known as the legendary wizard and the only survivor of the evil character Lord Voldemort.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is the best friend of Harry Potter and travels with him through his entire journey as a wizard.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is another best friend of Harry Potter. She is the smartest in her friend group and one of the favorite female characters in the series.

Professor Dumbledore

Professor Dumbledore is one of the most powerful wizards in the series and the man in charge of Hogwarts.
